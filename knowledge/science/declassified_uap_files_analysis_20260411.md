**Declassified Alien/UFO/UAP Files: Comprehensive Analysis (2026-04-11)**

### **1. U.S. Government Declassifications**
#### **A. Project Blue Book (1952–1969)**
- **Agency**: U.S. Air Force
- **Declassified**: 1970s–1980s (FOIA)
- **Key Findings**:
  - 12,618 UFO reports; 701 "unidentified" (~5.6%).
  - No extraterrestrial proof, but unexplained cases (e.g., **Kecksburg Incident**, **1952 D.C. flyovers**).
  - **Condon Report (1968)**: No national security threat; project shut down.
- **Archive**: [Blue Book Archive](https://www.bluebookarchive.org/)

#### **B. CIA UFO Documents (1940s–1990s)**
- **Declassified**: 1970s–present (CIA Reading Room)
- **Key Revelations**:
  - 1952 memo: UFOs real but not alien; likely Soviet tech or natural phenomena.
  - **Robertson Panel (1953)**: Recommended debunking UFOs to avoid panic.
  - **Stargate Project**: Remote viewing experiments included UFO-related espionage.
- **Archive**: [CIA UFO Collection](https://www.cia.gov/readingroom/collection/ufos)

#### **C. AATIP (Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program, 2007–2012)**
- **Agency**: DoD
- **Declassified**: 2017–present
- **Key Revelations**:
  - $22M black-budget program led by **Luis Elizondo**.
  - **Three declassified videos (2017–2020)**:
    1. **FLIR1 (2004)**: "Tic Tac" UAP (USS Nimitz).
    2. **GIMBAL (2015)**: UAP with no visible propulsion.
    3. **GOFAST (2015)**: Hypersonic UAP.
  - **2021 UAPTF Report**: 144 cases (2004–2021); 1 "unexplained" (potential breakthrough tech).
  - **2022 NDAA**: Mandated DoD UAP reporting; established **AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office)**.
- **Documents**: [2021 UAPTF Report](https://www.dni.gov/files/ODNI/documents/assessments/Prelimary-Assessment-UAP-20210625.pdf)

#### **D. AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office, 2022–Present)**
- **Agency**: DoD
- **Declassified**: 2022–2024
- **Key Revelations**:
  - **2023 Report**: 510 UAP reports (2022–2023); 171 "uncharacterized and unresolved."
  - Some UAPs exhibit **non-human intelligence signatures** (e.g., instantaneous acceleration, transmedium travel).
  - Reviewing **historical cases** (e.g., **1960s Minuteman Missile UFO incidents**).
- **Documents**: [2023 AARO Report](https://www.intelligence.gov/news/documents/2023-Annual-Report-on-UAP.pdf)

#### **E. NASA UAP Study (2022–2023)**
- **Agency**: NASA
- **Declassified**: 2023
- **Key Findings**:
  - No extraterrestrial evidence, but UAPs warrant scientific study.
  - Recommended better data collection (AI, satellites).
  - **Stigma barrier**: Pilots/scientists fear ridicule for reporting UAPs.
- **Documents**: [NASA UAP Report (2023)](https://science.nasa.gov/astrophysics/focus-areas/uap)

#### **F. 1960s Minuteman Missile UFO Incidents**
- **Declassified**: 2010s (FOIA)
- **Key Events**:
  - **1967 Malmstrom AFB**: UFOs **disabled 10 nuclear missiles** (confirmed by Air Force in 2010).
  - **1975 NORAD Incident**: UFOs triggered nuclear alert at Loring AFB.
- **Documents**: [Declassified Minuteman Files](https://www.theblackvault.com/documentarchive/ufos-and-nukes/)

#### **G. Wilson/Davis Memo (2002)**
- **Declassified**: 2019 (via To The Stars Academy)
- **Key Claim**:
  - Alleged briefing by **Dr. Eric Davis** to **Admiral Thomas Wilson** about **recovered non-human materials/craft**.
  - **Not officially verified** by DoD, but Davis confirmed authenticity in 2020.
- **Controversy**: Some call it a hoax; others see it as evidence of a black-budget recovery program.

### **2. International Declassifications**
#### **A. France (GEIPAN)**
- **Agency**: CNES (French Space Agency)
- **Declassified**: 1977–present
- **Key Cases**:
  - **2,000+ cases**; ~3% "Type D" (unexplained, high strangeness).
  - **1981 Trans-en-Provence**: Physical traces (burned soil, compressed vegetation) from UAP landing.
- **Archive**: [GEIPAN Database](https://www.geipan.fr/en)

#### **B. UK (MoD UFO Files)**
- **Agency**: UK Ministry of Defence
- **Declassified**: 2008–2013
- **Key Cases**:
  - **1980 Rendlesham Forest Incident**: U.S./UK military encountered metallic craft near RAF Woodbridge.
  - **1990s Condign Report**: UAPs are real but no threat; some "novel radar returns."
- **Archive**: [UK UFO Files](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/ufos)

#### **C. Canada (Project Magnet, 1950–1954)**
- **Agency**: Department of Transport
- **Declassified**: 1980s
- **Key Findings**:
  - Led by **Wilbert Smith**, who claimed UFOs used **electromagnetic propulsion**.
  - No smoking gun, but documents show government interest in UFO tech.

#### **D. Brazil (Operation Prato, 1977)**
- **Agency**: Brazilian Air Force
- **Declassified**: 2010
- **Key Event**:
  - **Colares Island UFO Wave**: Hundreds of sightings; **radiation burns** on witnesses.
  - Official conclusion: "Unconventional aircraft" of unknown origin.

#### **E. Mexico (2004 Air Force UFO Footage)**
- **Agency**: Mexican Air Force
- **Declassified**: 2004
- **Key Event**:
  - Infrared footage of **11 unidentified objects** near military aircraft.
  - No official explanation.

#### **F. Chile (CEFAA Files)**
- **Agency**: Chilean Air Force
- **Declassified**: 2010s
- **Key Cases**:
  - **2010 Chaitén Volcano UFO**: High-speed object on thermal camera.
  - **2014 "UFO Trail"**: Radar/visual sightings of object moving at **6,000 mph**.
